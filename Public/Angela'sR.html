Angela's Recipe for the best cinnamon rolls

Ingredients

For the dought:

  • % cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼teaspoon yeast
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼cup butter
  • 3 cups bread fluor

For the filling:

  • ⅔ cup dark brown suger
  • 1½ teaspoon yeast
  • ½ cup better

Intructions

  1. mix the milk with yeast, suger, eggs
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. add in the flour and mix until combined into a dough
  4. knead the dough for 10 min.
  5. tranfer the dough into a large bowl and cover with plastic wrap, leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours
  6. after the dough hads dough in size roll into a large rectangle.
